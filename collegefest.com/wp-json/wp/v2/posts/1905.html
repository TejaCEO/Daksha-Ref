Leap Day will bring us an extra bit of February this weekend, and we at CollegeFest think any student in Boston should take full advantage–you only get one Leap Day during your four college years, after all. Whether you want to toast the extra day, brush up on your history, or support some local businesses, our guide will help any Boston collegian make their Leap Day weekend worth a jump for joy<\/span>!<\/p>\n

(Photo By: DABE – East.Paxsite.com)<\/p><\/div>\n

Dr. Seuss Birthday Celebration<\/b><\/a><\/p>\n

Join the Harvard COOP and Harvard Square Business Association for a Dr. Seuss birthday party on Saturday. The free event includes readings, storytime, and green eggs & ham and birthday cake donated by local restaurants in commemoration of America\u2019s favorite author for kids of all ages. For aspiring rappers\/children\u2019s authors, this event could be a perfect opportunity to practice on-the-spot rhyming skills.<\/span><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Moving Images Film Fest<\/b><\/a><\/p>\n

Harvard Medical School sponsors the FREE Moving Images Film Fest, which will be held on Saturday at the Martin Conference Center. Explore a lineup of films and speakers aimed at breaking down the stigma about mental illness, disabilities, and other health issues.<\/span><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Leap Day Bar Crawl<\/b><\/a><\/p>\n

Stella Artois is sponsoring a Boston Bar Crawl in commemoration of the once-an-olympiad arrival of Leap Day. Discover some of Boston\u2019s most unique bars and take advantage of free giveaways. Event is 21+<\/span><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Disco Fridays @ The Sinclair<\/b><\/a><\/p>\n

Go to Motown free of cover charge this Friday at one of Cambridge\u2019s hottest venues. Join the Glitter Boys for a disco party. Event is 21+.<\/span><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Visit MassArt’s Newly Opened Museum<\/a>\u00a0<\/b><\/p>\n

MassArt\u2019s new museum had its grand opening just last week, and this weekend, you can get in the loop on one of the newest happenings in Boston\u2019s arts scene, completely free of charge. <\/span><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Support Chinatown Bingo<\/b><\/a><\/p>\n

Help our friends in Chinatown combat stereotyping and stigma by showing support to the thriving community and economy of one of Boston\u2019s liveliest neighborhoods. Chow down on some dumplings, find unique curios in Chinatown\u2019s shops, take in the area\u2019s striking public art, and learn about the community from local business owners.<\/span><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Black History Month Restaurant\u00a0Challenge<\/b><\/a><\/p>\n

Black History Month may be coming to an end this weekend, but there\u2019s still time to commemorate it in delicious fashion. Visit some of the featured Black-owned restaurants throughout Greater Boston before the month is up and treat yourself to meals from around the globe while supporting locally-owned eateries in the process.<\/span><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Jazz Scene in Boston @ Museum of African American\u00a0<\/strong>History<\/b><\/a><\/p>\n

The last few days of Black History Month can also be spent learning about the wide-ranging cultural contributions made by Boston\u2019s Black community at the Museum of African American History. The Museum\u2019s Jazz Scene in Boston exhibit will reveal the history of Beantown Jazz, delving into the storied venues, innovative sounds, and larger-than-life personalities that make up our city\u2019s jazz landscape.<\/span><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Ice Skating<\/b><\/a><\/p>\n

With a wintry chill expected to descend upon Boston this weekend, a trip to one of our city\u2019s many outdoor ice rink will make your Boston winter experience shivery and authentic. Just remember not to burn your tongue on the hot cocoa.<\/span><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

PAX East<\/strong><\/a><\/p>\n

Tickets are selling out fast for this weekend\u2019s PAX East, the hottest convention for indie gaming on the East Coast. Visit the Boston Convention Center to take a dive into the fantastical worlds of gaming, discover forward-thinking creators in the industry, and grab some awesome giveaways.<\/span><\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
