]]> What do you picture when you think about dining out in the city? I picture a flock of money emoji’s with wings and they are all flying out of my purse. If you picture something similar to this, then you probably find yourself staying in and preparing your own meals, because it seems like the cheaper option. For those nights when you just can’t find the time to cook, or need to grab something on the go, a few chain restaurants in Boston offer student discounts (participating locations may vary).

Tasty Burger: (Harvard Square) offers a $10 Starvin’ Student deal, which will get you a hamburger, fries and a beer.

Panda Express: (Prudential Center) 10% discount with student ID.

Tossed: (Prudential Center) 10% discount with student ID.

Burger King: 10% discount with college ID (participating locations may vary).

McDonalds: 10% discount with college ID (participating locations may vary).

Subway: 10% discount with college ID (participating locations may vary).

Weekly Deal Menu:

Monday: Fire + Ice Boston– $10.99 all you can eat Monday’s for college students.

Tuesday: The Squealing Pig (Roxbury Crossing)- Free Wellfleet Oysters 5-8 p.m.

Wednesday: The Draft(Harvard Ave., Allston)- $2 Taco Night & Trivia from 6pm-12am

Thursday: MiniBar (inside The Copley Square Hotel)- $2 sliders with bacon and cheese. 5-7 p.m.

Friday: Gem(42 Province Street)- .50 wings, 1/2 half priced pizza on Fridays

Saturday: Phoenix Landing (512 Mass Ave, Cambridge)- Half-priced appetizers from 5-7 p.m.

Sunday: Clarke’s (21 Merchant Row)- $5 Pizza and Beef Tacos specials, starting at 5 p.m.
